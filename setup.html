Setup

Setup is easy! Follow this step by step guide to help you get started!

Setup

To begin, install the Google Chrome extension. Once the extension is installed, a new icon will be created in your Chrome toolbar.

Then, install the Android app and open the app. A key will be presented to you. Leave this window open.

From there, we will need to set your master password and your key by right clicking the extension icon and selecting "Options".

Enter in a master password that you can remember, and type in the key presented to you by the Android app.

Account Registration

When you sign up for a site, go to the site and click on the lock icon. A popup window will appear giving you a secure password. Use this password as the password for your sign up. Click "Copy and Save" to save this password and to copy it to your clipboard to make signing up easier.

Once you have installed the extension to Google Chrome and the app to your Android device, and setup the various options, here's how easy it is to login to your sites using our system.

Go to the site and click the lock icon. A popup will appear asking for your master password.

After entering your master password, a barcode will appear. Open the Android app, click the "Scan" button, and scan the barcode using your Android device.

After entering your master password, a barcode will appear. Open the Android app, click the "Scan" button, and scan the barcode using your Android device.

A pin will appear. Enter your pin into the extension and the saved password will appear for you to use!
